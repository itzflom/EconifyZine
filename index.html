About the Zine
"We value critical thinking over certainty and clarity over complexity."

Econify Zine is a student-led youth publication dedicated to making economics more accessible, relevant, and engaging. We bridge the gap between academic economic theory and the real-world issues that shape our lives.

We publish student-written articles, commentaries, and analyses on current economic, political, and social developments. Through creative storytelling and critical thinking, we amplify youth perspectives in global economic discussions.

Our goal is not to provide definitive answers, but to equip young people with the tools and confidence to think for themselves — and to question the systems around them without fear.
